About

Hello! I’m Henil, a 3rd-year Computer Science student at UBC Vancouver with a love for immersive and visual technologies, and exploring new ways people interact with digital systems. I enjoy building interactive experiences that bring together software, design, and real-time visuals, whether through spatial interfaces, simulation, or experimental prototypes. Beyond that, I’ve built a wide range of full-stack applications, participated in hackathons and competitions, and supported younger developers in learning to make video-games :)

I’m currently working with my university’s Faculty of Medicine EdTech team to deliver XR learning experiences for medical learners across the province, and with the Centre of Digital Media to develop an immersive VR experience with Metro Vancouver that makes complex urban planning information more accessible to the public.

Currently seeking CO-OP/internship opportunities for fall 2026!
